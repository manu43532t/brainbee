We are excited to announce the launch of the official **Brain Bee Belgium** website!

This site is your one-stop resource for all information about:

- The **Belgian Brain Bee Championship**
- How to **register** as a student or school
- **Study materials** to help you prepare
- **News and announcements** about upcoming events
- How to get involved as a **teacher**, **scientist**, or **volunteer**

## What is the Brain Bee?

The International Brain Bee (IBB) is a neuroscience competition for high school students around the world. Belgium participates as part of this global network, sending our national champion to the International Brain Bee World Championship each year.

## Stay Connected
